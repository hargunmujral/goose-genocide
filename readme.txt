Description:

Gotta kill them all! In this action-packed game,
created on none other than a simple Python terminal,
take the role of "Campus Exterminator" to hunt and 
kill all the pesky geese invading UWaterloo!

Link to public repository: 

https://github.com/hargunmujral/goose-genocide

Instructions:

Click the goose in the centre to kill one unlucky
goose on campus! Collect powerups to increase the
efficiency of your extermination operation.
